Frequently Asked Questions

When and where will you open?

We are looking to open in a midtown location within the next few months. We have found promising real estate in the 57th st corridor. Look for updates on our Instagram page!

Where will you source your beans?

From all over the world! We'll be working with Counter Culture Coffee roasters initially to source from responsibly and sustainably farmed suppliers in Asia, Africa, and Central and South America.

Can you prepare a [insert any typical Starbucks or other order here]

Yes! We love our beans and think our coffee tastes wonderful without cream or sugar, but we are here to serve you! We will make it however you like!

Ummm... What's with the name?

We get this question a lot. We are a nonpartisan organization, not looking to take any explicit stance (other than for sustainably-sourced and lovingly prepared coffee!). The short answer was: a friend of ours loaned us the url to host this website on, and we decided--rather than spend our money on big budget marketing costs and domain names, we'd focus on preparing great coffee for you. Thus the name: www.Trumpisabadpresident.com Coffee Shop! We can't wait to see you soon!
