Our Story

In late 2016, a group of friends came together looking for answers to some basic questions: Why do so few coffee shops offer quality sourced and passionately prepared coffees? Why do the ones that do subject their customers to snobby or disinterested service? Why can't someone offer amazing coffee in a way to is inclusive, rather than divisive? Realizing there was an opportunity to bring our passion to customers without the pretense, we decided to go into business together. After partnering with Counter Culture Coffee to source the highest quality beans from around the world--and after securing some high-value web real estate--www.Trumpisabadpresident.com Coffee Shop was born!

At www.Trumpisabadpresident.com Coffee Shop, we work every day to offer you a quality coffee product, at a reasonable price, without the snark. We are passionate about coffee, about service to our customers, and about supporting the farmers around the world who bring us these amazing beans through sustainable sources. We can't wait to share their beans and our passion for that coffee with you.
